Reward Catalogue

Your reward options are endless. Choose from Merchandise, Gift Cards, Tickets, Experiences, Expedia, Give Back – and more. Explore and discover rewards just for you.

While the catalogue changes depending on the options selected for your programme and the region where you work, everything you can see in Reward Catalogue is available for you.

Viewing the Catalogue

You can access the Reward Catalogue by going to the Catalogue tab when you are logged in to your programme. Note: The name of the catalogue tab may be different for your programme.
